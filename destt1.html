Mahabhlaeshwar

Mahabaleshwar, nestled in the lush Western Ghats of Maharashtra, is a breathtaking hill station known for its cool climate, scenic landscapes, and historical significance. Located about 120 km from Pune and 260 km from Mumbai, it serves as a popular retreat for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking for a peaceful escape..
